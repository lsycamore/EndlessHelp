How to play

Start menu

Ok, so first of all you will be greeted by the start menu. This menu contains the options for starting the game and even quitting the game (if you dare!). 

Gameplay

Moving the character

Most important thing is to move the character to play the game. When the game starts you will start moving straight away. You will be able to jump to avoid the incoming obstacles. To jump you will need to just simply tap on the screen. If you want to jump for longer just hold your finger on the screen.

Death

Important thing, so when you come into contact with an obstacle you will instantly die. This will open up the death interface which is mentioned further below.

Score

You will receive points based on the longest time survived in game. If you decide to leave the game, dont worry, your high score will still be saved.

User interface

Ok so there will be two interfaces that you will or may deal with:

Pause menu

This menu is initiated by pressing that button in the top right of your game. This will give you the option to restart the game losing all progress, quit to the main menu and also to resume from where you left off.

Death menu

This menu will pop up as soon as you come into contact with any of the obstacles, This will only give you two options: restarting the game or quiting to the menu menu.
